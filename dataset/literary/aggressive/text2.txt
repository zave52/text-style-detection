The violent, unforgiving hurricane slammed into the fragile coastal settlement with the terrifying, unbridled fury of a vengeful demon released from the darkest depths of the ocean. Towering waves, black as midnight and foaming with toxic white froth, crashed mercilessly against the stone breakwaters, instantly obliterating the massive granite blocks into thousands of jagged, deadly projectiles. The howling wind did not merely blow; it screamed, a deafening, high-pitched shriek that violently tore the wooden roofs from the fishermen's cottages and sent sharp wooden splinters rocketing through the chaotic air like deadly shrapnel. Nature itself had become a sadistic, bloodthirsty predator, intent on erasing every single trace of human civilization from the map. Ancient oak trees were brutally uprooted, their massive root systems tearing from the saturated soil with a sickening, violent crack before being tossed aside like worthless twigs. The relentless, punishing torrential rain felt like a barrage of icy needles, slicing aggressively into the exposed flesh of anyone foolish enough to remain outside. The storm possessed a dark, malevolent sentience, a brutal force of sheer destruction that ruthlessly battered the crumbling walls, absolutely determined to smash the village into unrecognizable debris.
