The blood-soaked earth trembled beneath the thundering hooves of a thousand heavily armored warhorses, their iron-shod hooves mercilessly churning the battlefield into a gruesome paste of mud and shattered bone. He stood atop the jagged ridge, his dark eyes burning with an unquenchable, venomous hatred that seemed to scorch the very air around him. Every muscle in his battered body coiled with explosive, lethal tension as he watched the cowardly enemy forces attempting to retreat toward the burning tree line. With a deafening, feral roar that tore from the deepest depths of his lungs, he raised his heavy broadsword high above his head, the jagged steel catching the crimson light of the dying sun. He did not want a mere strategic victory; he demanded absolute, catastrophic annihilation. He charged down the steep incline with terrifying ferocity, swinging the massive blade with such devastating, brutal force that it cleaved effortlessly through iron shields and the desperate men trembling behind them. The metallic clash of weapons and the agonized screams of the dying became a chaotic, deafening symphony of pure violence. He was a wrathful, unstoppable god of war, determined to drown the entire opposing kingdom in a boundless ocean of their own blood.
