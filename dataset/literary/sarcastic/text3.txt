The infamous, dreaded fortress of the Dark Lord loomed terrifyingly over the desolate valley, striking absolute fear into the hearts of men, primarily because it looked like a stiff breeze could collapse the entire structure. The impenetrable obsidian walls were a masterpiece of evil architecture, assuming the architect's primary goal was to create a building with a truly astonishing number of structural leaks. A magnificent, blood-chilling moat surrounded the castle, cleverly filled with completely stagnant, foul-smelling swamp water that bred a highly aggressive species of mildly annoying mosquitoes. The elite, terrifying guards stationed at the heavy iron gates demonstrated their unparalleled vigilance by being loudly asleep, their thunderous snoring echoing across the bleak landscape. Inside, the grand throne room was a breathtaking display of sinister power, expertly decorated with moth-eaten tapestries and a terrifyingly wobbly chandelier that threatened to assassinate anyone who stood beneath it. It was truly a marvel of dark sorcery that the supreme master of all evil managed to strike terror into the realm while actively struggling to keep his own ceiling from caving in on his head on a daily basis.
