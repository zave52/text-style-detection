The freezing, merciless ocean water was rising with terrifying speed inside the lower decks of the shattered galleon, already swirling dangerously around their waists as they desperately clawed at the jammed wooden hatch. They had to breach this locked door instantly, or they would be permanently trapped in a dark, watery grave at the bottom of the sea. He swung the heavy iron crowbar with a wild, panicked ferocity, striking the rusted padlock repeatedly as the icy water rapidly surged up to their freezing chests. Time was completely evaporating; the main hull was groaning in absolute agony, threatening to split entirely in half and drag them all down into the crushing abyss. Her trembling, numb fingers frantically searched the submerged debris for anything that could act as a lever, her heart hammering a frantic, explosive rhythm against her ribs. With one final, desperate, muscle-tearing heave, the rusted metal latch finally snapped in two, and they violently pushed the grate open, scrambling desperately toward the upper deck just as the ocean fully consumed the hold.
