The ancient stone pillars groaned in sheer agony as the cataclysmic tremor ripped violently through the subterranean cavern, sending massive chunks of the vaulted ceiling crashing dangerously close to their heels. She sprinted blindly through the choking clouds of pulverized rock, her lungs burning with every desperate, jagged breath as the structural integrity of the entire temple rapidly disintegrated around them. She tried to scream for him to run faster, but her terrified voice was instantly swallowed by the deafening, thunderous roar of the earth tearing itself apart. The heavy iron door marking the only remaining exit was slowly grinding shut, triggered by the ancient, unforgiving security mechanism. They had less than thirty seconds before thousands of tons of crushing granite permanently sealed them inside this lightless, suffocating tomb. He grabbed her arm, his grip bruising in his absolute desperation, and violently hurled them both toward the rapidly narrowing gap of the exit. The absolute terror of the impending collapse pushed their exhausted bodies past every conceivable human limit. They threw themselves across the threshold just as the colossal stone door slammed shut with a sickening, final thud, leaving them gasping in the dust.
