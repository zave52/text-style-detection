Our executive board recently announced an ambitious transformation project designed to modernize company operations for the future. Employees were informed that adaptation is essential, especially since several departments will now handle the workload previously assigned to twice as many people. Leadership described the decision as a strategic optimization effort rather than what everyone else calls obvious understaffing. During the town hall meeting, executives enthusiastically praised resilience, collaboration, and dedication while standing beside a presentation explaining why annual bonuses would unfortunately not be possible this year. Thankfully, the motivational background music softened the disappointment slightly.
