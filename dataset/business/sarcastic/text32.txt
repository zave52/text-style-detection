The human resources department proudly unveiled a new initiative aimed at improving workplace satisfaction and employee retention. According to the presentation, the solution involves mandatory “fun engagement activities” scheduled during lunch breaks so productivity remains unaffected. Staff members can now participate in motivational quizzes, trust exercises, and carefully monitored discussions about positivity. Naturally, suggestions involving salary adjustments were classified as financially unrealistic. Management believes morale can be significantly improved through inspirational newsletters, despite overwhelming evidence that people generally prefer money over digital optimism.
