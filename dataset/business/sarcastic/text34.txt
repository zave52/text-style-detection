The operations division introduced a revolutionary productivity framework this week, promising faster workflows and improved efficiency across all departments. Curiously, the new process involves additional meetings, expanded reporting requirements, and several layers of managerial approval before simple decisions can be finalized. Employees were assured that these adjustments would reduce stress, although most people appeared significantly more stressed immediately after hearing the announcement. Leadership continued emphasizing the importance of balance and mental wellness while simultaneously encouraging teams to remain available after working hours for “urgent collaborative opportunities.” Corporate logic truly remains one of humanity’s greatest mysteries.
