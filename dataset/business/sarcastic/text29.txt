The marketing team proudly revealed a bold new campaign designed to “redefine customer engagement.” Translation: the logo became larger, the slogans became vaguer, and the budget somehow disappeared overnight. During the presentation, executives repeatedly emphasized innovation while recycling ideas from competitors that were already outdated two years ago. Still, everyone applauded enthusiastically because questioning the strategy would require admitting that thousands of dollars were spent inventing a different shade of blue for the company website.
