The strategic relocation of our primary manufacturing and regional distribution center is currently proceeding according to the established logistical timeline. The external contracting firm has officially completed the structural reinforcement of the new facility and finalized the installation of the automated conveyor systems on the main warehouse floor. Commencing next week, the logistics division will begin systematically transferring the existing raw material inventory from the temporary storage units to the permanent facility. This transition will be executed in highly coordinated phases to ensure that current production quotas remain unaffected and outstanding client orders are fulfilled without any administrative delays. The designated facility managers will conduct comprehensive safety inspections of the newly installed operational hardware before authorizing full-scale manufacturing activities. All warehouse personnel will receive updated security badges and standardized parking permits from the human resources department upon their arrival at the new location. The complete transition is expected to conclude by the end of the current operational quarter, at which point the legacy warehouse lease will be formally terminated.
