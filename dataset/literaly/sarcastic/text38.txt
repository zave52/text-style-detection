I must write to express my profound admiration for the spectacularly agonizing seating arrangements in your incredibly opulent new drawing room. To commission a massive sofa meticulously carved from solid granite, deliberately angled to perfectly ensure the human spine is contorted into a posture of ceaseless torment, is an inspired stroke of sociopathic interior design. I marveled at how swiftly the lively conversation withered into a grim, stoic silence as each guest desperately attempted to mask their overwhelming physical pain beneath a quite thin veneer of polite aristocratic etiquette. You have successfully created a space so breathtakingly unwelcoming that a damp dungeon floor would honestly feel like a luxurious sanctuary by comparison.
