It is immensely generous of the royal physician to prescribe such a breathtakingly revolting tincture for my mild evening headache. The distinct flavor of crushed beetles combined with stagnant swamp water is truly a culinary revelation that distracts me completely from the dull initial pain. Now, instead of merely suffering a minor head ache, I am fully consumed by the majestic struggle to keep my very last meal from making a dramatic reappearance upon the exceptionally expensive woven silk rug.
