I'm absolutely spellbound by the revolutionary new carriage you have commissioned for the grand royal procession. The bold decision to replace the standard, highly functional wooden wheels with perfectly solid blocks of imported marble is an undeniably striking choice. As the vehicle is mercilessly dragged across the cobblestones, the deafening screech of grinding stone provides a truly delightful symphony that guarantees our beloved sovereign will arrive at the glorious festivities with a profoundly shattered spinal cord and absolutely zero of his remaining hearing capabilities.
