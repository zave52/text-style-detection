The grand portrait you have unveiled of our esteemed Duke is a breathtaking assault on the foundational principles of visual arts. I am particularly captivated by your daring choice to render his noble visage in the sickly, mottled hues of a decomposing eggplant. The fact that his left eye appears to be frantically searching for an escape route off the edge of the canvas only adds to the profound emotional depth of the piece. You have masterfully ensured that no future heir will ever gaze upon this masterpiece without experiencing a sudden, overwhelming urge to immediately surrender their inherited titles and flee into the wilderness.
