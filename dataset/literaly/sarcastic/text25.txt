I must truly commend the architect for designing a fortress that is so impeccably vulnerable to any passing breeze. The decision to construct the outer defensive walls entirely from ornamental frosted glass is a stroke of sheer tactical brilliance. I am quite certain that when the heavily armed barbarian hordes finally arrive to inevitably siege this majestic stronghold, they will be so overwhelmed by the sheer aesthetic beauty of their own reflections that they will simply lay down all their weapons.
