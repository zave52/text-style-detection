Inside the magnificent botanical greenhouse, the humid air hangs heavy with the overwhelming scent of blooming orchids and damp earth. Condensation streaks the curving glass panels above, blurring the sharp outlines of the external world. Enormous tropical ferns uncurl their vibrant green fronds over the narrow gravel pathways, creating a dense, jungle-like canopy. Water drips unceasingly from a rusted iron pipe into a stone basin entirely covered in moss.
