The sun sets violently behind the towering red rocky mesas, casting long, dramatic shadows across the arid riverbed. The scorched earth cracks into intricate geometric patterns, entirely devoid of any visible moisture or resilient desert vegetation. A sudden gust of wind sweeps through the narrow canyon walls, carrying fine grains of abrasive sand that scour the exposed sandstone formations. High above, a solitary hawk circles the thermal currents, scanning the barren expanse for the slightest sign of movement before nightfall overtakes the landscape.
