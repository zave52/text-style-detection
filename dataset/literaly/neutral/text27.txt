Perched on the very edge of a sheer cliff, the astronomical observatory overlooks a vast expanse of dark, unpopulated terrain. The massive silver dome reflects the brilliant starlight, sliding open with a low mechanical rumble to reveal the powerful telescope housed inside. Inside the control room, glowing monitors display endless columns of complex telemetry data, casting a sterile blue hue over the empty swivel chairs. Absolute silence rules this remote altitude, completely separated from the endless noise of the deep valley below.
