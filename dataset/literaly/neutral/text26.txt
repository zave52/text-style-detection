The sprawling urban park lies motionless beneath the pale, diffused light of the early morning dawn. Thick mist clings tenaciously to the surface of the artificial lake, entirely obscuring the ornamental bridge and the surrounding willow trees. Wet blades of grass bend slightly under the considerable weight of the morning dew, while empty iron benches wait silently along the paved walkways. Distant traffic produces a low, constant hum, serving as the only reminder of the waking metropolis beyond these quiet gates.
