Nestled deep within the snow-capped mountain range, the isolated village remains largely untouched by modern civilization. Wooden chalets with steeply pitched roofs cluster tightly together, their stone chimneys continuously exhaling plumes of fragrant woodsmoke. Narrow, winding streets are paved with uneven cobblestones, polished smooth by generations of foot traffic. At the center of the village stands an ancient oak tree, its thick branches sheltering a simple iron bench.
