The subterranean subway station echoes with the distant, metallic screech of an approaching train, vibrating through the cold, tiled floors. Fluorescent lights flicker unreliably overhead, illuminating the faded advertisements plastered unevenly against the curved concrete walls. Turnstiles stand perfectly frozen, their metal arms locked in place, while a layer of accumulated dust covers the vacant ticket booths. Despite the cavernous scale of the transit hub, the thick, stagnant air feels intensely claustrophobic, preserving an unsettling stillness between the arrivals.
