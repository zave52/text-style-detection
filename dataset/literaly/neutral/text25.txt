The abandoned industrial factory looms silently over the polluted river, its massive brick walls stained with decades of black soot and chemical residue. Shattered windows line the upper levels, allowing the harsh wind to howl uninterrupted through the vast, empty production floors. Rusted gears and broken conveyor belts lie scattered amid the accumulating rubble, slowly being reclaimed by invasive vines. A lone feral cat darts across the cracked concrete, vanishing into the encroaching shadows.
