Flee the twisted woods, for the ancient beast of the hollow has awakened from its slumber! I heard the deafening crack of timber and the guttural roar that freezes the blood in one's veins. It is hunting by scent, crashing through the ancient pines with ravenous fury. Drop your heavy packs and extinguish all lanterns; the light will only draw its terrible gaze. We must reach the silver river before it catches our scent. Sprint for your lives!
