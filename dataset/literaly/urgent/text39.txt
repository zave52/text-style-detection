The boiling ocean has turned black as pitch, and the colossal leviathan has finally breached the abyssal depths! Wake the islanders immediately; the beast's shadow alone eclipses our grandest towers, and its deafening roar shatters the stained glass of our chapels. A giant tidal wave, born from the creature's violent ascent, is racing toward the harbor with apocalyptic speed. Abandon the fishing fleets and leave the cargo behind; gold will not buy your salvation from the jaws of a mythic god! Flee toward the volcanic peaks and seek safe refuge within the high mountain caves. The very earth is fracturing beneath our desperate feet. Move without hesitation, for the monster's gaping maw threatens to consume this entire fragile island and drag us all to a watery doom!
