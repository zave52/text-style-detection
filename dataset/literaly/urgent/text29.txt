The slumbering mountain has violently ruptured, vomiting rivers of molten fury upon the terrified valley below! A suffocating cloud of toxic sulfur and raining ash eclipses the sun, plunging us into a premature, apocalyptic dusk. Gather the frightened children and abandon the harvest; your earthly possessions mean nothing to the advancing tide of liquid fire. We must ascend the western ridge immediately. One misstep, and the earth will swallow us!
