The colossal stone dam above our sleeping town is groaning under the weight of the swollen, torrential river! I saw the massive granite blocks shifting, weeping water through a hundred fresh and terrifying cracks. Wake the children and ring the cathedral bells now! We have mere moments before the mighty structure bursts, unleashing a wrathful tsunami that will scour the valley clean of all human life. Leave the gold and livestock! Scramble up the steep muddy embankments to the ancient fortress on the high hill. Do not look back at your cherished homes! The deafening roar of the breaking reservoir is the herald of doom, and the ravenous floods will show no pity to the slow!
