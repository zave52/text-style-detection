The pristine silence of the frosty peaks has been violently shattered by a sudden, deafening crack! A colossal wave of blinding white death is hurtling down the jagged slopes, devouring ancient pines. Wake the slumbering villagers and sound the brass alarm bells! We must abandon the cozy hearths now. Do not pause to gather your silver or heavy winter cloaks; the suffocating snow cares for no mortal. Run swift with the icy wind at your back! If that roaring avalanche catches us in the lower valley, we shall all be entombed in a frozen, breathless grave for the rest of eternity! Move your tired feet, I beg of you!
