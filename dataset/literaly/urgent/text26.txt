Barricade the heavy oak doors and seal the windows with pitch immediately! The weeping plague has breached the lower slums, and its creeping miasma is drifting toward our sanctuary. Bind your mouths with cloth steeped in vinegar and herbs. Listen to the agonized cries echoing through the cobblestone streets; that is the sound of inescapable death. Do not let anyone inside, not even your kin, or the rot will consume us all before morning!
