A monstrous tempest of golden sand is rising on the western horizon, blotting out the cruel desert sun! Whip the exhausted camels and abandon the heavy chests of silk; our lives depend on reaching the cave. The wind shrieks like a choir of vengeful spirits, carrying jagged grains that will flay the flesh from our bones. Wrap your linens tightly around your mouth and nose! To breathe the churning dust is to invite a slow, agonizing suffocation. We must navigate by the jagged peaks before the blinding maelstrom obscures the path. There is no sanctuary out here in the dunes. Run now! The howling fury of the desert is upon us, and it hungers for our souls!
