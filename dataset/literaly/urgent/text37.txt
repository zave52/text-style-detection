The macabre painting is warping, and the grotesque figures within have broken their gilded frame! I watched in sheer horror as the cursed painted canvas tore open like rotting flesh, spilling black blood upon the stones. Flee this haunted manor and bolt the stout doors from the outside! Do not let those jagged, wicked claws drag you into their painted hellscape. They hunger for the warmth of living souls to replace their fading colors! Cover the remaining mirrors and snuff out the candle flames; light only helps them track our frantic movements. We must reach the holy sanctuary before the stroke of midnight. If the clock chimes and we are still trapped in these corridors, we shall become part of the cursed portrait forever!
