In accordance with the recently updated corporate governance framework, all active employees are hereby notified of the upcoming modifications to the central data retention policy. Effective at the commencement of the third fiscal quarter, all localized digital files exceeding a storage threshold of fifty gigabytes must be successfully migrated to the primary cloud infrastructure. This procedural adjustment is specifically designed to optimize the performance capabilities of the internal network and ensure strict compliance with federal data protection regulations. The information technology department will systematically deploy automated archival software to all registered workstation terminals to facilitate this comprehensive transition. Personnel are required to manually verify the successful transfer of their respective departmental archives prior to the scheduled deadline. Any digital assets remaining on local servers after the migration period will be subject to automated deletion to free up necessary server capacity. Please direct any technical inquiries regarding this mandatory procedure to the designated support personnel within the compliance division.
