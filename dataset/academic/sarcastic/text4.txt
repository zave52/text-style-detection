We must collectively marvel at the sheer intellectual bravery demonstrated during Dr. Arrington’s keynote address at this year’s symposium on advanced quantum mechanics. It takes a deeply extraordinary level of confidence to stand before an audience of esteemed international physicists and proudly present a theoretical model that essentially reinvents the wheel, only this time making it significantly less round. The audience was undeniably captivated by his revolutionary decision to spend forty-five minutes monotonously reading directly from slides packed with entirely illegible, completely unformatted equations. His profound refusal to provide any actual empirical evidence to support his wild cosmological claims was a refreshing departure from the tiresome, traditional reliance on actual scientific proof. Furthermore, his remarkable ability to skillfully dodge every single clarifying question during the panel discussion by simply repeating his own abstract verbatim was an absolute masterclass in academic evasion. It was truly an unforgettable presentation, leaving the entire auditorium utterly speechless, primarily because every single attendee had lapsed into a deep, inescapable state of profound scholarly catatonia.
