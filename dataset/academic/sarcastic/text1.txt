We are truly humbled to have been given the unparalleled opportunity to review this spectacular manuscript, which bravely challenges the oppressive tyranny of basic empirical evidence. The authors have remarkably managed to write a fifty-page dissertation without accidentally stumbling upon a single coherent thought, an achievement that surely requires an extraordinary level of dedication to intellectual mediocrity. We must express our profound awe at their highly innovative methodology, which apparently consists of completely ignoring decades of established scientific consensus in favor of wild, unsubstantiated guessing. It is deeply refreshing to see a research team so boldly liberated from the tedious constraints of statistical significance, choosing instead to rely entirely on what can only be described as academic hallucination. The stunning lack of a proper literature review is clearly a deliberate stylistic choice designed to emphasize their complete isolation from the scientific community. We eagerly recommend this masterpiece for immediate publication, preferably in a journal dedicated to experimental fiction, where its complete lack of factual accuracy will be properly appreciated.
