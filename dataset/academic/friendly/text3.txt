Welcome, everyone, to the annual symposium on advanced computational methodologies! We are so incredibly thrilled to have such a vibrant and diverse community of scholars joining us from universities all around the world today. Whether you are a distinguished senior professor or a first-year student attending your very first academic conference, please know that you are a highly valued and warmly welcomed member of this community. Our primary goal for this weekend is to foster a deeply supportive, inclusive, and genuinely friendly environment where we can safely share our ideas, celebrate our collective successes, and learn from our shared challenges. I strongly encourage you all to introduce yourselves to someone new during the coffee breaks, ask highly curious questions during the panel discussions, and most importantly, have a wonderful time connecting with your fellow researchers. We have arranged a beautiful welcome reception for this evening, and I am so deeply looking forward to speaking with as many of you as possible. Thank you so much for being here and bringing such wonderful energy!
