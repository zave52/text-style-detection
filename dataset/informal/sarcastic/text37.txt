You somehow move through life with the confidence of a motivational speaker and the planning skills of someone assembling furniture without instructions, patience, or basic survival instincts. Honestly, every interaction feels like watching a reality show where the main character refuses to learn lessons because self-awareness would ruin the entertainment value. I especially admire how you can create a problem, complain dramatically about the problem, reject every reasonable solution offered, and then act personally victorious when the situation resolves itself accidentally three days later. That level of dedication to unnecessary struggle is honestly inspiring in the same way natural disasters are technically impressive from a safe distance.
