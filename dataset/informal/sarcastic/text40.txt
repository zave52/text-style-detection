Being around you feels like participating in a social experiment designed to measure how much confusion one person can create while still acting completely convinced of their own brilliance. Honestly, it’s a remarkable performance. You approach every conversation with the confidence of someone delivering groundbreaking wisdom, even when your argument collapses under basic questions almost immediately. Still, I respect the consistency. Most people eventually experience doubt, embarrassment, or at least temporary self-awareness after making obvious mistakes, but you continue forward like accountability is a fictional concept invented by jealous people. I especially admire how every bad outcome somehow becomes proof that the world failed to appreciate your “vision” rather than evidence of terrible decisions.
