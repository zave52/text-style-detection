Yesterday evening, the internet connection suddenly stopped working for almost an hour, and everyone at home immediately became annoyed. At first, I kept checking the router every few minutes, hoping the connection would return quickly. After a while, though, I just gave up and started reading a book instead. Surprisingly, the time passed much faster that way, and by the moment the internet returned, I was already more interested in finishing the chapter. Sometimes unexpected breaks from technology are more useful than people expect them to be.
