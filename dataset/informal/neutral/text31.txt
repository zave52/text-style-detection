A few evenings ago, I decided to clean my computer because it had become really slow during the last few weeks. At first, I thought the process would take maybe twenty minutes, but then I noticed how many unnecessary files, screenshots, and old downloads were still saved there. I ended up organizing folders, deleting programs I never used, and even updating a few applications that had been ignored for months. It actually felt satisfying once everything was finished. The laptop runs much better now, and I also realized how much digital clutter builds up when you keep postponing simple tasks for too long without noticing it.
