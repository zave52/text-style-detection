Lately, I have been trying to spend less time checking notifications on my phone. I noticed that even quick breaks can easily turn into long distractions when every app wants attention at the same time. Now I usually leave the phone on the other side of the room while studying or working. It feels strange at first, but after a while it becomes easier to focus without constantly looking at the screen every few minutes. The difference is not huge, but my daily routine feels calmer now.
