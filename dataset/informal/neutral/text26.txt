A few days ago, I started listening to an old playlist that I had not opened in years. It was interesting how many memories came back just from hearing certain songs again. Some tracks reminded me of long trips, while others brought back random moments from school or late-night conversations with friends. Music really has a strange way of connecting people to specific moments, even when a lot of time has already passed since then. I did not expect such a simple thing to completely change my mood for the entire evening.
