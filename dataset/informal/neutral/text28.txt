Recently, I started waking up a bit earlier than usual just to avoid rushing through the morning. Before that, I was always trying to get ready as fast as possible, which made the start of the day feel stressful for no reason. Now I have enough time to drink coffee slowly, check messages, and plan what needs to be done. It is a small change, but it honestly makes everyday routines feel much more manageable overall. I still do not enjoy waking up early, but at least mornings feel less chaotic and more organized than before.
