I spent most of my Sunday afternoon trying to assemble that new wooden bookshelf I ordered online a few weeks ago for the living room. The delivery driver dropped off the heavy cardboard box on my front porch yesterday, but I was way too tired after work to even think about opening it up until today. I unpacked all the different wooden panels and laid out the metal screws on the floor so I could clearly see what I was working with before reading the instruction manual. The diagrams were a little bit confusing at first, mostly because they did not explicitly label which side of the boards were supposed to face outward, but I eventually managed to figure it out by looking at the reference picture on the box. It took me about two hours of slowly putting all the pieces together and tightening the bolts with a small wrench to finally get the whole structure standing completely upright. I moved it over to the empty corner next to the television and started organizing all my old textbooks and video game cases on the different shelves. The room looks significantly more organized now that I actually have a proper place to store all those loose items.
