The weather forecast mentioned that it was probably going to rain for most of the afternoon, so I made sure to pack my heavy waterproof jacket and an umbrella before leaving the apartment today. I took the bus down to the city center instead of walking, mostly because I did not want to risk getting all my paperwork soaked if a sudden thunderstorm started while I was halfway there. The ride was fairly quiet since it was past the usual morning rush hour, and I just listened to a podcast about history while looking out the window at the passing cars. By the time I finished my appointment and walked back out onto the street, the dark clouds had actually cleared up entirely and the sun was starting to shine through. Since it turned out to be such a surprisingly nice and clear afternoon, I decided to take the long way home and walk through the public park to get some fresh air. There were a lot of people out walking their dogs or just sitting on the benches reading books. I eventually stopped at a small convenience store near my building to buy a bottle of water before heading back upstairs to finish the rest of my homework for the evening.
