Not long ago, I started watching a documentary series during dinner instead of scrolling through social media feeds. At first, I only planned to watch one episode occasionally, but it quickly became a relaxing evening habit. Some episodes are about technology, others focus on travel, history, or unusual professions people have around the world. What I like most is that the stories feel calm and informative without demanding too much attention after a long day. It is also interesting how quickly you can learn random facts from topics you normally would never search for on purpose. Compared to endlessly checking notifications or short videos, this routine feels slower and more balanced, which honestly makes evenings much more enjoyable overall.
