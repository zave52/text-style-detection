Last Friday, I met with a few friends at a small cafe that recently opened near the city center. None of us expected anything special from the place, but the atmosphere there was surprisingly comfortable and quiet compared to the louder spots we usually visit. We stayed for several hours just talking about random things, sharing stories, and discussing plans for the summer. It was one of those evenings where time passes quickly without anyone noticing. Even after leaving, the conversation continued outside while we slowly walked through the streets. Moments like that are simple, but they often end up becoming the most memorable parts of an ordinary week.
