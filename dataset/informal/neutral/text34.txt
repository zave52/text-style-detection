This week, I tried changing my daily schedule a little by doing important tasks earlier instead of leaving everything for the evening. Normally, I keep postponing things until later because it feels easier to relax first, but then I end up feeling tired and unmotivated by the end of the day. Surprisingly, finishing work sooner gave me much more free time afterward, and the evening felt calmer because there was nothing stressful left to complete. It is funny how small adjustments can completely change the mood of the entire day. I still do not follow the routine perfectly, but even partial progress already feels more effective than my old habits that depended entirely on last-minute motivation.
